# Organization Context Template

Fill this in **once**, keep it **generic**, and reuse it for every report. This is what makes Stage 3's
relevance call useful — and keeping it generic is what keeps you safe with cloud AI tools.

> ✅ Include: sector, rough size, broad platform categories, general exposures.
> ❌ Never include: internal hostnames, IP addresses, account names, specific product/tool/vendor names,
> ticket data, secrets, or anything non-public. You do not need any of it for a good relevance assessment.

- **Security capability (broad):** (e.g. small internal SOC; EDR on most endpoints; email filtering)
- **Crown jewels (general categories):** (e.g. customer records, payment systems, source code)
