## When a report becomes a leadership question

Briefing a report is one job. A very different one starts when an executive reads the news and asks:

> "Are **we** exposed to this — and what are we doing about it by Friday?"

That's not a summary. It's a decision-support task under time pressure: decompose the question, plan
collection, weigh competing explanations, calibrate confidence with intelligence-grade language, and
deliver an answer leadership can act on **plus** an analyst annex that holds up to scrutiny.
