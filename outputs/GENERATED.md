# About this folder

`outputs/` holds the **deliverables you generate** when you run the kit — primarily
`internal-threat-brief.md`, your finished one-page brief.

- The `internal-threat-brief.md` currently here is the **synthetic worked sample**. When you run the kit on
  your own report, replace it with your brief (or save yours alongside it with a dated name, e.g.
  `internal-threat-brief-2026-06-21.md`).
- Files here are **generated working artifacts**, not canonical product source. Edit them freely.
- Keep your own briefs' source links and confidence notes intact so they stay defensible later.
